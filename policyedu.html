Are your products safe for newborns?

Our products are formulated for children aged 2 and above. For newborns and infants under 2 years, we recommend consulting your paediatrician before use. Our gentle formulas are free from harsh chemicals, but every baby's skin is unique.

How often should I use your hair growth products?

For best results, we recommend using our hair growth system 2–3 times per week. Consistency is key — you should begin seeing noticeable improvements within 4–6 weeks of regular use.

Do you ship outside Nairobi?

Yes! We deliver across Kenya, including Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret, and all upcountry destinations. Delivery times vary by location, typically 2–4 business days outside Nairobi.

Can I pay on delivery?

Yes, we offer pay-on-delivery for orders within Nairobi. For upcountry orders, we require M-PESA payment in advance. We accept M-PESA, Visa, and Mastercard.

Are your products cruelty-free?

Absolutely. Glow & Flowless products are never tested on animals. We are proud to be a cruelty-free brand committed to ethical, sustainable practices throughout our supply chain.

What if my child has a sensitive scalp?

All our products are formulated for sensitive scalps. We use gentle, natural ingredients and avoid sulfates, parabens, and artificial fragrances. We recommend doing a patch test before full application if your child has a history of skin sensitivities.
